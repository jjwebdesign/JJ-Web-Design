About JJ Web Design

JJ Web Design is a locally owned and operated business located in Parksville, BC. Founded by Vancouver Island University Computer Science Student and aspiring entrepreneur Jasper Charlinski. Based on the philosophy "the simpler the better" JJ Web Design produces simple and easy to use yet effective and efficient web pages that are usable on all device. Our webpages do not collect cookies, account information, or search history. They have one purpose, and that is to be a platform for you to display your message
