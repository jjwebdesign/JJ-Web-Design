Main Color

This will be the color that shows up the most on your webpage, pick a color that best represents your websites message.

Secondary Color

This will be the color that shows up the second most on your webpage, pick a color that complements your main color.

Tertiary Color

This will be the color that shows up the third most on your webpage, pick a color that complements your secondary color.

Outline Color

This will be the color that will outline certain sections and text.

Use the text boxes bellow to fill out the information for each section.

About

This section should be a short summary of what your website is about.

Service

This section should be a list of the services and or products you are offering, this should include a short description of the service/product as well as pricing options.

Contact

This section should contain your desired contact information.

Custom

This section can be for what ever you think best fits. You have the choice between an order / inquiry page, a image slideshow or video, or a regular text section. If you have an idea of something else you might want let me know and I will try an make it happen!

Footer

This section is typically used for social media links or similar information, but can be filled with anything.
